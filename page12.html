ABOUT THE FOUNDER 

Emily John MSN, APRN, FNP-C

Our founder, Emily John, was raised in Tampa. After completing high school at Academy of the Holy Names, she went on to receive her Bachelor’s of Science in Nursing at Saint Louis University in Saint Louis, Mo. She completed her Master’s of Science in Nursing at the University of South Florida.

Emily’s nursing career started in the neonatal ICU and expanded to plastic surgery as both a bedside Nurse and Family Nurse Practitioner.

NICU NURSE 
As a neonatal ICU nurse, Emily loved educating her parents and families on the best newborn care. Although, she felt the anxiety of her patient's parents as they transitioned home as a new family. In 2018, Emily gave birth to her daughter, Evelyn, and life as she knew it changed. Being a mom gave Emily an even greater understanding of the difficult transition a family experiences as they bring a new baby home. She personally knows the patience and grace it takes to take care of a newborn while still taking care of yourself.  

PLASTIC SURGERY NURSE
 As a plastic surgery nurse, Emily was constantly seeing the effects of stress on her patient’s overall surgical outcome. Clinical studies show the negative impact of stress on the wound healing process. There are essential aspects of care that are consistently being overlooked by the patient due to anxiety and the overwhelming nature of surgery.
