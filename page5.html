POST-OPERATIVE SERVICES

SURGICAL SUPPORT

We will help coordinate all of your care with your surgeon including transportation to and from your surgery, prescription pick-up and meals, do wound checks and foley/drain care.

TRAVEL COORDINATION

For our out of town patients, we can help coordinate all of your travel needs such as airline tickets, hotel, and transportation.

PATIENT ADVOCACY

We can help interpret all the medical jargon for you and your family members to make sure you receive the best possible care. As nurses, we know how to navigate the healthcare system quickly.
