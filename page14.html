ABOUT CCN

The feeling of vulnerability is undeniable as a patient or family caregiver. Navigating the medical world can be extremely stressful. The fear of the unknown or seeing your loved one in such a vulnerable state is hard. While preparing for surgery or giving birth, there is an overload of information that the patient or family member is responsible for remembering. With the team at Coastal Concierge Nursing, we act as your support system to reduce stress and promote optimal healing. Coastal Concierge Nursing is all about peace of mind during the surgical experience.
